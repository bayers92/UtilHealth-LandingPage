Our Specialities

We work the top specialists in their respective fields to determine what data is clinically relevant and can help inform delivery of care. No more sifting through endless rows of irrelevant data - we give you the numbers you want in an easily digestable forum that won't interupt your workflow.

Chronic Disease

Chronic diseases are expensive and difficult to manage effectively. UtilHealth gives physicians, registered dietitians, and other specialists a more effective way to help patients achieve their goals and improve outcomes cost-effectively through increased communication and data capture.

Recovery

With the increasing prevalence of bundled payments, hospitals and surgeons are becoming responsible for all costs associated with an entire episode of care. By better tracking patient activities and compliance, care teams can proactively intervene when necessary to reduce hospital readmissions and save costs.

Population Health

In an era of Accountable Care Organizations and capitated payments, provider networks need more effective tools for managing the health of their patients in a cost-effective way. UtilHealth leverages technology to give providers actionable insights to reduce costs and improve outcomes.
